Why Social Media May Be A Dentriment to Our Society


Whether you hate it or love it, Social Media has began to rise higher and higher. However, this has started a string of events that are starting to impact our views and ideas. Let's take a look.

There have been cases, that, believe it or not, have caused relationships to end or stirred feelings of jealousy and inner insecurity just because of a simple post. According to Pew Research Center, 34% of 18-19 year olds have shared mixed feelings and thoughts of jealousy because of their partners habits or reactions to other users on Social Media. Even an accidental or intentional like from an Ex has caused partners to snap onto their significant others.
